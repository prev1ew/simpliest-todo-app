Product Info

Product name: web app - ‘BeFocused’

Industry: Education

Product Type: To-do list, task manager

Main Business Domains: Education

Product Vision

Here, at RubyGarage we know how important it is to constantly upgrade your skills. Achieving a lot without losing focus is the main goal of any self-education process. We are sure that people never stop learning and we really want to help those who thrive for knowledge. All of us are students and learners along our path in life. That’s why we want to introduce an app - ‘BeFocused’. 

 

App - ‘BeFocused’ is a convenient centralized personal space for any students, learners, or others interested in self-education and self-development. It empowers users to set goals, track them and complete them without losing focus.

 

Users can easily control their progress, see what’s up to be done next and see how much time is left by seeing the due dates. It allows them to plan their education, tasks, and progress.

 

FOR Students, learners

WHO wants to keep track of their educational goals and manage them

THE app ‘BeFocused’

IS A convenient to-do list

THAT  allows creating, editing, deleting, and managing goals that the user wants to achieve

UNLIKE

  • Keeping track of the goals in the notebook
  • Memorizing all of them

OUR PRODUCT

  • Allows to easily see all incomplete & complete goals in one centralized list
  • Set priorities and due dates which help manage the educational process
  • Mark goals as ‘Complete’ to see which goals are not achieved yet
  • Provides the possibility to share one’s own educational goals with friends easily
